What I Do

Quality issues on major projects are rarely caused by lack of procedures.

They happen when systems don't translate into execution. My work focuses on closing that gap.

  • Establishing ISO 9001-aligned quality management systems that are practical, not theoretical
  • Structuring submittal, inspection, and approval workflows for clarity and control
  • Driving NCR reduction through data-led root cause analysis
  • Building KPI dashboards that reflect real project performance
  • Aligning contractors, consultants, and PMC teams under a unified quality approach
