03_My Approach

Most projects treat quality as a control function. I approach it as a system.

That means:

Looking beyond individual NCRs to identify patterns and systemic gaps
Shifting from reactive corrections to preventive control
Creating visibility through structured, decision-focused reporting
Ensuring quality processes support delivery — not slow it down
Most quality systems don't fail because they are incomplete. They fail because they are not used the way they were designed.
